September 24 Starts a New Class of The Iron Yard Greenville Students
By Gary Robinson, Student at The Iron Yard Greenville
Happenings and updates from The Iron Yard in Greenville, SC
SEARCH

The Iron Yard kicked off another semester of class this past Monday in Greenville, SC. Lead by instructor Jacob Smith, students settled into what will be one of the greatest challenges they have ever faced. Future developers will apply what they will learn in class and deliver functional software that pushes their ability to the limit.
